# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic

In this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. The aim was to explain the importance of digital literacy in a simple and visual way.

The infographic includes topics such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools for students, and the importance of maintaining a professional online presence. I focused on making the design clear and easy to understand so that it can help students quickly learn key concepts.

One difficulty I faced was organizing all the information in a limited space while keeping the design clean. However, this task helped me understand how visual content can effectively communicate important ideas.
