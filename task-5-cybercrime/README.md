# Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

In this task, I learned about cybercrime and how it affects people in real life. Cybercrime includes activities like phishing, hacking, and online fraud, where attackers try to steal personal information.

One common example is phishing, where a person receives a fake email or message that looks real. If someone clicks on the link or shares their details, their data can be misused.

To stay safe from cybercrime, it is important to follow some basic precautions. We should not share passwords or OTPs with anyone, avoid clicking on unknown links, and always use strong passwords. It is also important to check the authenticity of emails and websites before trusting them.

This task helped me understand how easily cybercrime can happen and why it is important to be careful while using the internet.
