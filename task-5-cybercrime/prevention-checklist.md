# Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

## Stay Safe Online

- Do not share passwords or OTP with anyone
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links
- Always check the website URL before entering details
- Use strong passwords with letters, numbers, and symbols
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Keep your apps and devices updated
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for important work

## Financial / UPI Safety

- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
- Always verify payment requests before sending money

## Reporting Cybercrime

- Report at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
