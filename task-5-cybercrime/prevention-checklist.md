# Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

## Stay Safe Online

- Do not share your passwords or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links received via email, SMS, or social media
- Always check the website URL carefully before entering any personal details
- Use strong passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts
- Keep your apps, phone, and laptop updated to avoid security risks
- Do not use public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive activities
- Be cautious of messages creating urgency or fear (like account blocked alerts)

## Financial / UPI Safety

- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone under any circumstances
- Always check whether you are sending or receiving money before approving any request

## Reporting Cybercrime

- Report at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
