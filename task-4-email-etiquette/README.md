# Task 4 – Email Etiquette

In this task, I learned how to write formal and professional emails. I wrote two emails, one for requesting information about an assignment and another for applying for an internship opportunity.

While doing this task, I understood that writing a proper email is important because it creates the first impression. A good email should have a clear subject, polite greeting, proper structure, and respectful closing.

I also created a social media checklist that includes do’s and don’ts for responsible online behavior. This helped me understand how our actions on digital platforms can affect our image.

Overall, this task helped me improve my communication skills and understand the importance of maintaining professionalism in digital communication.
