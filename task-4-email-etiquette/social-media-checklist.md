# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Use a professional profile photo
- Keep your bio clear and meaningful
- Share useful and positive content
- Maintain privacy settings
- Be respectful in comments and posts

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal information publicly
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread fake news
- Avoid using weak passwords
- Do not engage in online arguments
